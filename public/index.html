Can I pay via M-Pesa? +
Yes! The Selar checkout accepts M-Pesa Paybill, Visa, and Mastercard. Once payment is confirmed you'll receive the download link instantly via email — usually within seconds.
What exactly are the 14 templates and how many sheets are there? +
The 14 templates cover: P&L Statement, KRA VAT Return, Kenya Payroll Register, M-Pesa Reconciliation, Chama Tracker, Cash Flow Tracker, Stock Management, Farm Income Tracker, Personal Budget, VAT Invoice, Project Cost / BOQ, Break-Even Analysis, Debt Tracker, and PAYE Calculator. Each template has 2–5 detailed analysis sheets — totalling 44 individual worksheets across the full library.
Are the 2025 KRA tax rates (NSSF, SHIF, Housing Levy) already in the templates? +
Yes. The payroll template uses the 2024 NSSF Act (Tier I + Tier II at 6%), SHIF at 2.75% of gross (replacing NHIF), Affordable Housing Levy at 1.5%, and the current 5-band PAYE scale with KES 2,400 personal relief. VAT is set at 16%. All rates are updated whenever KRA makes changes — Business tier gets 2 years of updates, Enterprise gets lifetime updates.
Do the templates work with Google Sheets? +
Yes — all templates are fully compatible with Microsoft Excel (Windows & Mac) and Google Sheets. Some formatting will look slightly different in Google Sheets but all formulas work correctly. A compatibility guide is included.
I'm not an Excel expert. Can I still use these? +
Absolutely. The templates are colour-coded so you only fill in the yellow/blue cells — everything else calculates automatically. Each template also includes an Instructions sheet that walks you through exactly what to do step by step.

All three packages include the same 14 templates. The difference is: Starter is for 1 user with 6 months of updates; Business covers up to 10 users, 2 years of updates, and priority WhatsApp support; Enterprise has unlimited users, lifetime updates, custom company branding, and a 1:1 Zoom customisation session.
